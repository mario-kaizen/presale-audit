Creative Notes — Direction 2

This direction requires real Edmonton content as the buildout progresses. These mockups use Kelowna photography as proxies. Swap in real Edmonton imagery for authenticity as it becomes available.

Why this works: Documentary-style content consistently outperforms polished brand ads in Phase 1. People follow the journey. By the time the studio opens, they feel like they were part of building it.

Content needed from Marta: Weekly buildout photos/videos, a short owner introduction video (60-90s, iPhone is fine), any renders or plans of the Edmonton layout.

Creative Notes — Direction 3

Numbers verified: 500+ members and 5.0★ Google rating (362 reviews) confirmed as of March 2026. Testimonial copy is placeholder — use real Google reviews from Kelowna with permission.

Recommended mix: Run all three directions simultaneously. Direction 1 (Announcement) as the lead. Direction 2 (Buildout) as ongoing organic + boosted. Direction 3 (Social Proof) as retargeting for people who've already seen the announcement.

A/B tests: Class-through-window vs plank-with-instructor for engagement. Stats overlay vs testimonial for conversion. Branded lifestyle shot (wine bottle) vs class action for retargeting.
