Class Types
Choose your burn.
STRONG Body
Our OG STRONG class. Full-body Pilates-inspired training paired with cardio bursts. Each session is designed to push limits and build your strength, mobility, and endurance.
STRONG Loaded
Built for progress. Focused, structured and designed around true progressive overload. Deliberate, full-body strength work with a lower or upper bias using heavy springs and dumbbells.

Pilates, strength, and cardio in one dynamic, full-body session. Each week brings a new challenge and fresh format designed to build strength, movement and resilience.

The best of traditional Pilates with strength-based movements. Controlled, deliberate sequences build strength, balance, and mobility while delivering that signature STRONG burn. Progressive overload built into each training block.

Dynamic movements and Pilates flows designed to enhance strength, stability and mobility. Core strength with the use of props to deepen muscle engagement and precision.
