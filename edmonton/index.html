Phase 1 Deep Dive
Build the Audience — 7 Weeks
The goal is simple: 800–1,200 leads in the database before we sell anything. CPL target: under $6 CAD.

What We're Running

  • Meta Ads (Facebook + Instagram) — "Coming Soon" awareness campaigns targeting Edmonton locals within 15km of studio location
  • Lead form ads — low-friction "Get Early Access" with name, email, phone, suburb
  • Retargeting — video viewers and page visitors see follow-up creative
  • Organic content — buildout progress, owner introduction, behind-the-scenes, community teasers

Audience Targeting

  • Primary: Women 25–45 within 15km — fitness, wellness, pilates interests
  • Secondary: Lookalike audiences from Kelowna's existing member list
  • Exclusion: Existing Kelowna members (prevent cross-contamination)
  • Geo: Edmonton metro area — tight radius, not province-wide

Creative Strategy

  • Hook: "Something new is coming to Edmonton" — curiosity-driven, not offer-driven
  • Brand video: STRONG reformer footage + Edmonton-specific text overlays
  • Buildout content: Real photos/videos of the space being built — creates anticipation
  • Social proof: Kelowna member testimonials repurposed for Edmonton awareness

CRM & Automation (Grow)

  • Lead capture: Meta lead form → Grow contact → tagged "Presale Lead"
  • Welcome sequence: Immediate SMS + email confirmation on registration
  • Nurture drip: Weekly buildout updates, owner story, "what is STRONG?" education
  • Pre-sell warm-up: Last 2 weeks of Phase 1 tease the intro offer coming in Phase 2
Why no offer yet? The data from 6 presales shows that studios who build a warm database first convert at 2–3x the rate of studios that lead with offers. Phase 1 is about trust and anticipation — the selling happens in Phase 2.

Landing Page Notes

Built in Grow (GHL page builder). Form submits directly to Edmonton Grow account, tags "Presale Lead", triggers welcome automation. Dark-on-dark design matches STRONG's brand identity — no white backgrounds.

"What is STRONG" section is critical. Edmonton has zero STRONG awareness. People need to understand this isn't regular reformer pilates — the ROW/RIDE/REFORM breakdown does that in 3 seconds.

Stats bar builds credibility. 100+ studios, 37K+ members, 14 countries — this isn't a startup. It's a global brand arriving in their city.

"Down for more?" Uses the brand's secondary tagline as the bottom CTA. On-brand and direct.

Icons: All icons in the final build should be custom SVGs — no emojis. Clean line icons matching STRONG's minimal aesthetic (black/white, consistent stroke weight).

Suburb field: Critical for Phase 2 geo-targeting. Also helps Marta understand her catchment area.

Time to talk about the thing that makes STRONG different from every other Pilates studio, and honestly, from every other workout you've tried.

Picture a Pilates reformer. Now attach a rower to it. That's the Rowformer. Half reformer, half rower, fully engineered to do things neither can do alone. It's exclusive to STRONG. You won't find it anywhere else.

And then there's the Bikeformer. Same idea, but with a stationary bike integrated into the reformer. Same exclusivity.

Rowformer close-up — beauty shot, moody lighting

You'll choose from three formats:

• ROW. Rowformer-based. Cardio meets resistance in a way that makes 45 minutes feel like 20.
• RIDE. Bikeformer-based. Low-impact cycling fused with reformer work.
• REFORM. Pure reformer. Classical Pilates principles, STRONG programming.

Class types:

• STRONG Body. Our OG STRONG class. Full-body Pilates-inspired training paired with cardio bursts.
• STRONG Loaded. Built for progress. Deliberate, full-body strength work with heavy springs and dumbbells.
• STRONG 360. Pilates, strength, and cardio in one dynamic, full-body session. Fresh format every week.
• Pilates Strength. The best of traditional Pilates with strength-based movements. Progressive overload built in.
• Pilates Only. Dynamic movements and Pilates flows for strength, stability and mobility.

Every class is 45 minutes. Low-impact, high-intensity. Edmonton will have 12 Rowformers and 6 Bikeformers. A full studio, every class.
