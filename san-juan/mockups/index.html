Creative Notes — Direction 2

This direction requires real San Juan content as the buildout progresses. These mockups use Proscenium photography as proxies. Swap in real San Juan imagery for authenticity as it becomes available.

Why this works: Documentary-style content consistently outperforms polished brand ads in Phase 1. People follow the journey. By the time the studio opens, they feel like they were part of building it.

Content needed from Kenneth: Weekly buildout photos/videos, a short owner introduction video (60-90s, iPhone is fine), any renders or plans of the San Juan layout.
