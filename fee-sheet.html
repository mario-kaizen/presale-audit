How It Works — Americas

The 16-Week Roadmap

Four phases counting down to opening day. Marketing pipeline and studio sales track run in parallel.

W-12 → W-9
Build the Audience

Low-spend lead generation. "Coming soon" positioning. Build email, SMS, and retargeting audiences. $300–500/week ad spend. Target: 800+ leads.

W-8 → W-4
Sell Intros + Upsell VIP

Launch intro offer campaigns. Ramp spend to $500–1,000/week. Retarget Phase 1 leads. Email/SMS nurture pushing both intro and VIP offers. Target: 150+ intros, 50+ VIP members.

W-3 → W-1
Push to Opening

Peak spend: $1,000–2,000/week. Maximum urgency. Scarcity messaging. Daily email/SMS in final week. Target: 400+ intros, 100+ VIP members.

→ Opening Day
W+1 → W+4
Convert the Pipeline

Post-opening support. Retarget intro buyers. Conversion sequences. Spend tapers to $300–700/week. Target: 200+ total members.
