How It Works

The 16-Week Roadmap

Four phases counting down to opening day. The presale model sells intro offers first, then converts them to memberships post-opening.

W-12 → W-9
Build the Audience

Low-spend lead generation. "Coming soon" positioning. Build email, SMS, and retargeting audiences. $300–500/week ad spend. Target: 800+ leads in the pipeline.

W-8 → W-4
Sell Intros + Upsell VIP Members

Launch intro offer campaigns. Ramp spend to $500–1,000/week. Retarget Phase 1 leads. Email/SMS nurture pushing both intro and VIP membership offers. Target: 150+ intros sold, 50+ VIP members locked in.

W-3 → W-1
Push to Opening

Peak spend: $1,000–2,000/week. Maximum urgency and scarcity messaging. Daily email/SMS in final week. Target: 400+ intros sold, 100+ VIP members secured.

→ Opening Day · 400+ intros · 100+ VIP members
W+1 → W+4
Convert the Pipeline

Intro buyers attend their first classes and convert to full memberships. Retargeting campaigns and conversion sequences. Spend tapers to $300–700/week. Target: 200+ total active members.
