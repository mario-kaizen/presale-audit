How It Works

The 16-Week Roadmap

Four phases counting down to opening day. In APAC, the presale sells memberships directly — no intro offer layer. The goal is 200+ members signed up before the doors open.

W-12 → W-9
Build the Audience

Low-spend lead generation. "Coming soon" positioning. Build email, SMS, and retargeting audiences. $300–500/week ad spend. Target: 600+ leads in the pipeline.

W-8 → W-4
Sell Memberships

Launch presale membership campaigns with VIP founding-member pricing. Ramp spend to $500–1,000/week. Retarget Phase 1 leads. Email/SMS nurture driving direct membership sign-ups. Target: 80+ memberships sold.

W-3 → W-1
Push to Opening

Peak spend: $1,000–2,000/week. Maximum urgency — VIP pricing closes at opening. Scarcity messaging with live member count. Daily email/SMS in final week. Target: 200+ memberships sold.

→ Opening Day · 200+ members signed up
W+1 → W+4
Fill Remaining Capacity

Post-opening campaigns at standard pricing. Retarget unconverted leads. Testimonial and social proof content from opening week. Spend tapers to $300–700/week. Target: grow beyond 200 baseline.
